Exercices : Division de fractions

Version professeur

Exercice 1. (2 points) Donner l'inverse de : a) 7 b) $\dfrac{4}{3}$ c) $-\dfrac{5}{2}$ d) 0,5

Correction. a) $\dfrac{1}{7}$ b) $\dfrac{3}{4}$ c) $-\dfrac{2}{5}$ d) $\dfrac{1}{0,5} = 2$.

Exercice 2. (2 points) Calculer $\dfrac{5}{6} \div \dfrac{2}{3}$.

Correction. $\dfrac{5}{6} \times \dfrac{3}{2} = \dfrac{15}{12} = \dfrac{5}{4}$.

Exercice 3. (2 points) Calculer $4 \div \dfrac{3}{7}$.

Correction. $4 \times \dfrac{7}{3} = \dfrac{28}{3}$.

Exercice 4. (2 points) Un gateau de $\dfrac{3}{4}$ kg est partage en parts de $\dfrac{1}{8}$ kg. Combien de parts ?

Correction. $\dfrac{3}{4} \div \dfrac{1}{8} = \dfrac{3}{4} \times 8 = 6$ parts.

Exercice 5. (3 points) Calculer $\dfrac{7}{12} \div \dfrac{14}{9}$.

Correction. $\dfrac{7}{12} \times \dfrac{9}{14} = \dfrac{63}{168} = \dfrac{3}{8}$.

Exercice 6. (3 points) Calculer $A = \dfrac{2}{5} \times \dfrac{3}{4} \div \dfrac{6}{10}$.

Correction. $A = \dfrac{2}{5} \times \dfrac{3}{4} \times \dfrac{10}{6} = \dfrac{60}{120} = \dfrac{1}{2}$.

Bareme total : 14 points.
