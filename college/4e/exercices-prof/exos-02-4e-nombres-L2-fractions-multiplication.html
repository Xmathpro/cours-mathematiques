Exercices : Multiplication de fractions

Version professeur

Exercice 1. (2 points) Calculer : a) $\dfrac{2}{3} \times \dfrac{5}{7}$ b) $\dfrac{4}{9} \times \dfrac{3}{8}$ c) $\dfrac{7}{10} \times \dfrac{5}{14}$

Correction. a) $\dfrac{10}{21}$ b) $\dfrac{12}{72} = \dfrac{1}{6}$ c) $\dfrac{35}{140} = \dfrac{1}{4}$

Exercice 2. (1 points) Calculer $A = 5 \times \dfrac{3}{8}$.

Correction. $A = \dfrac{15}{8}$.

Exercice 3. (2 points) Les $\dfrac{3}{4}$ des 32 eleves d'une classe ont reussi le controle. Combien ?

Correction. $\dfrac{3}{4} \times 32 = \dfrac{96}{4} = 24$ eleves.

Exercice 4. (2 points) Calculer $\left(\dfrac{3}{5}\right)^2$.

Correction. $\dfrac{3}{5} \times \dfrac{3}{5} = \dfrac{9}{25}$.

Exercice 5. (3 points) Simplifier $\dfrac{8}{15} \times \dfrac{5}{12} \times \dfrac{9}{4}$.

Correction. $\dfrac{8 \times 5 \times 9}{15 \times 12 \times 4} = \dfrac{360}{720} = \dfrac{1}{2}$.

Exercice 6. (2 points) Un terrain rectangulaire mesure $\dfrac{7}{2}$ km sur $\dfrac{4}{5}$ km. Calculer son aire.

Correction. Aire = $\dfrac{7}{2} \times \dfrac{4}{5} = \dfrac{28}{10} = \dfrac{14}{5}$ km$^2$ = 2,8 km$^2$.

Bareme total : 12 points.
