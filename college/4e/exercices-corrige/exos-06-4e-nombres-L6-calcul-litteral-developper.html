Exercices : Calcul litteral : developper

Corrige

Exercice 1. (2 points) Developper : a) $5(2x + 3)$ b) $-3(x - 4)$ c) $x(2x - 7)$.

Correction. a) $10x + 15$ b) $-3x + 12$ c) $2x^2 - 7x$.

Exercice 2. (2 points) Developper et reduire $(x + 2)(x + 6)$.

Correction. $x^2 + 6x + 2x + 12 = x^2 + 8x + 12$.

Exercice 3. (2 points) Developper et reduire $(3x - 5)(2x + 1)$.

Correction. $6x^2 + 3x - 10x - 5 = 6x^2 - 7x - 5$.

Exercice 4. (2 points) Developper et reduire $A = 3(x + 4) - 2(x - 1)$.

Correction. $A = 3x + 12 - 2x + 2 = x + 14$.

Exercice 5. (3 points) Montrer que $(x + 3)^2 = x^2 + 6x + 9$.

Correction. $(x+3)(x+3) = x^2 + 3x + 3x + 9 = x^2 + 6x + 9$.

Exercice 6. (3 points) Developper $(2x + 1)(3x - 4) - (x + 2)(x - 3)$.

Correction. $(6x^2 - 8x + 3x - 4) - (x^2 - 3x + 2x - 6) = 6x^2 - 5x - 4 - x^2 + x + 6 = 5x^2 - 4x + 2$.
