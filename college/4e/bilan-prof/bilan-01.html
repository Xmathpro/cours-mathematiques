Bilan 1er trimestre

Lecons 01, 02, 03, 04, 05, 06, 07 . Duree : 55 minutes

Objectif : evaluation sommative. Calculatrice autorisee.

Exercice 1. (2 points) Calculer : a) $(-6) \times 5$ b) $(-4) \times (-7)$ c) $8 \times (-3)$ d) $(-2) \times (-9)$

Correction. a) $-30$ b) $28$ c) $-24$ d) $18$

Exercice 2. (2 points) Calculer : a) $\dfrac{2}{3} \times \dfrac{5}{7}$ b) $\dfrac{4}{9} \times \dfrac{3}{8}$ c) $\dfrac{7}{10} \times \dfrac{5}{14}$

Correction. a) $\dfrac{10}{21}$ b) $\dfrac{12}{72} = \dfrac{1}{6}$ c) $\dfrac{35}{140} = \dfrac{1}{4}$

Exercice 3. (2 points) Donner l'inverse de : a) 7 b) $\dfrac{4}{3}$ c) $-\dfrac{5}{2}$ d) 0,5

Correction. a) $\dfrac{1}{7}$ b) $\dfrac{3}{4}$ c) $-\dfrac{2}{5}$ d) $2$.

Exercice 4. (2 points) Calculer : a) $3^4$ b) $(-2)^5$ c) $10^{-3}$ d) $(-1)^{100}$.

Correction. a) $81$ b) $-32$ c) $0{,}001$ d) $1$.

Exercice 5. (2 points) Ecrire en notation scientifique : a) $56\,000$ b) $0{,}0042$ c) $700 \times 10^5$.

Correction. a) $5{,}6 \times 10^4$ b) $4{,}2 \times 10^{-3}$ c) $7 \times 10^7$.

Exercice 6. (2 points) Developper : a) $5(2x + 3)$ b) $-3(x - 4)$ c) $x(2x - 7)$.

Correction. a) $10x + 15$ b) $-3x + 12$ c) $2x^2 - 7x$.

Exercice 7. (2 points) Factoriser : a) $8x + 12$ b) $5x - 20$ c) $x^2 + 7x$ d) $6x^2 - 9x$.

Correction. a) $4(2x+3)$ b) $5(x-4)$ c) $x(x+7)$ d) $3x(2x-3)$.

Bareme total : 14 points.
