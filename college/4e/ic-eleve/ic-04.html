Interrogation courte 04

Factorisation et equations (lecons 07, 08) . Duree : 15 minutes

Exercice 1. (2 points) Factoriser : a) $8x + 12$ b) $5x - 20$ c) $x^2 + 7x$ d) $6x^2 - 9x$.

Exercice 2. (2 points) Factoriser $A = 15x^2 + 10x$.

Exercice 3. (2 points) Resoudre : a) $4x + 5 = 21$ b) $7x - 3 = 25$ c) $-2x + 9 = 1$.

Exercice 4. (2 points) Resoudre $6x - 1 = 2x + 11$.
